# Technical Writing Samples

This section includes selected writing samples from CIS311 Technical Writing in CIS. These samples demonstrate research, organization, audience awareness, technical explanation, and professional documentation.

## Technical Proposal

**Background:** This assignment focused on proposing a practical technical solution for a workplace or organizational need.

**Approach:** I organized the proposal around the problem, audience, recommended solution, benefits, and implementation considerations.

**Results:** The final document demonstrated persuasive technical communication and professional proposal structure.

**Next Steps:** I would continue improving this type of document by adding stronger visuals, clearer cost details, and more measurable success criteria.

## Long Technical Report

**Background:** This assignment required research, organization, source documentation, and a professional report structure.

**Approach:** I used a formal structure with an introduction, body sections, evidence, analysis, and conclusion.

**Results:** The report helped demonstrate my ability to explain technical information clearly for a professional audience.

**Next Steps:** I would strengthen future reports with more visuals, executive summaries, and clearer recommendations.

## Instructional or Procedural Document

**Background:** This assignment focused on explaining a process clearly for users.

**Approach:** I organized the instructions step by step and considered audience needs, clarity, usability, and accessibility.

**Results:** The final document showed how technical writing can make complex tasks easier to follow.

**Next Steps:** I would improve future instructions by adding screenshots, labels, and troubleshooting notes.
